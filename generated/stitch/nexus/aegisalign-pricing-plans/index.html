Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan directly from your dashboard. Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.

What counts as a 'Node'?

A node is any individual server, container, or cloud endpoint being monitored and secured by the AEGIS Protocol.

Is there a setup fee for Enterprise?

Enterprise plans include personalized onboarding. Setup fees are typically waived with annual commitments.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes, we offer specialized pricing for non-profits and educational institutions. Contact our support team for details.

What happens if I exceed my node limit?

We provide a 10% grace margin. If you consistently exceed limits, our team will reach out to help you scale to the next tier.

How secure is my data?

AegisAlign uses AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are SOC2 Type II compliant.
